Writing

Holo-Sync is not just another mock interview platform — it is a biometric interview training system designed to simulate real-world pressure.

Unlike traditional platforms that focus only on answers, Holo-Sync analyzes how a candidate behaves under stress. Using technologies like remote photoplethysmography, eye tracking, and posture analysis, our system detects heart rate changes, eye contact, and body language in real time.

Instead of making interviews easier, Holo-Sync prepares candidates for reality. The system maintains or increases difficulty under pressure, just like real interviewers, and identifies the exact moments where the candidate loses composure.

After each session, users receive a detailed performance breakdown, including stress spikes, confidence drops, and behavioral patterns. This allows them to understand not just what they answered, but how they performed under pressure.

Our goal is not to comfort candidates, but to train them to handle real interview situations with confidence, awareness, and control.
